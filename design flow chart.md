Algorithm:
Start the program

Accept an integer from user

B) FLOWCHART FOR FINDING LARGEST OF THREE NUMBERS
Problem: Accept three integers and display the largest among them

Algorithm:
Step 1 :Start the program

Step 2 :Accept three integers (num1, num2, num3)

step 3 : Compare num1 with num2 and num3

step 4 :If num1 is greater than both, display num1 as largest

step 5 :If not, compare num2 with num3

step 6 :If num2 is greater than num3, display num2 as largest

step 7 :If not, display num3 as largest

step 8 :End the program
